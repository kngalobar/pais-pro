The PAIS Score

A verdict, not just a calculation.

The PAIS Score is a structured 100-point framework across six categories — Financial Viability, Market Quality, Physical Condition, Operational Management, Location, and Strategic Fit. Weighted by your investment mandate. Scored by AI against current market data. Adjustable by you.

It produces one of four verdicts: Strong Buy, Qualified Buy, Further Diligence, or Decline. That verdict travels with the deal into every downstream document.

Platform Features

Built for the deal, not the demo.

Every feature exists because a working syndicator needed it. None of it is vaporware.

// AI Intake

Extract. Don't retype.

Paste an OM excerpt, a broker email, a Zillow listing, or a raw rent roll. Upload PDFs, Excel files, or images. PAIS Pro reads everything and populates every deal field automatically. Then you add to it as the deal evolves.

// Pro Forma

Model the potential, not just the present.

Unit-by-unit rent roll with current and market rents. 10-year cash flow projection with your assumptions. IRR, equity multiple, DSCR, sensitivity analysis. Excel export for lenders. This is the model, not a summary of it.

// IC Memo

LP-ready in seconds.

Investment committee memorandum generated automatically from everything you've entered. Professional capital markets language. PAIS Score and verdict embedded. Share via link or export as PDF. No Word document. No midnight formatting.

// Lender Workflow

From analysis to application in one step.

Live financing rates (SOFR, Freddie Mac PMMS, private money ranges). Curated lender directory filtered by loan type, geography, and size. One-click loan application forwarding with your deal package attached. Referral fee tracking built in.

// Offering Documents

From analysis to offering. No attorney required for the draft.

AI-generated PPM, subscription agreement, and investor questionnaire from your deal data. Reg D 506(b), 506(c), 4(a)(2), Regulation S. Disclaimer watermark enforced. Attorney-review language embedded. Per-document pricing from $149.

// Deal Marketplace

List any PAIS-scored deal on the TaureanAI deal marketplace. PAIS composite score visible on the listing — a quality signal no other deal board provides. Display-only. No broker-dealer required. $499 per listing.
